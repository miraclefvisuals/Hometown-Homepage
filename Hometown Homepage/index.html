About Ado-Ekiti

Ado-Ekiti, the capital city of Ekiti State in southwestern Nigeria, is a vibrant hub known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and scenic landscapes. Nestled among rolling hills and lush greenery, Ado-Ekiti offers a unique blend of traditional and modern experiences.
The city is home to several notable landmarks, including Ero Dam and Ikogosi Warm Springs, where visitors can enjoy natural beauty and tranquility. With bustling local markets, lively festivals such as the Ekiti Parapo Festival, and a warm, welcoming community, Ado-Ekiti is a destination that captivates both residents and visitors alike.
Whether exploring its historical sites, engaging in outdoor activities, or savoring traditional cuisine, Ado-Ekiti provides a memorable experience that reflects the heart and soul of Ekiti State.
